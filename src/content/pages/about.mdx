I’m Swas, a product-focused engineer who likes building useful things with calm systems behind them.

My work has moved across WordPress products, web platforms, developer tooling, system architecture, and practical AI workflows. I founded [WPQuark WebTech Private Limited](https://wpquark.com), built commercial WordPress products like [eForm](https://eform.live), and have spent a lot of time turning messy product requirements into maintainable software.

I started with PHP, MySQL, jQuery, CSS, HTML, WordPress themes, and plugin development. That background still shapes how I think: software should be understandable, operationally boring, and friendly to the people who need to change it later.

These days I spend more attention on product engineering, React and web platforms, Node.js, developer experience, system design, and AI-assisted engineering that remains practical enough to trust.

This site is my personal notebook for ideas that deserve more than a thread and less than a book.

- Practical AI systems and AI-assisted engineering.
